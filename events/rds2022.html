Welcome to the 2022 edition of the GEODES Research Day and Symposium. This event aims to bring together current and former members of GEODES to exchange research results, ideas and plans, as well as reflecting on the previous year and preparing for the year ahead.

Coordinates

Date: January 21 (Friday), 2022. (To be confirmed in December.)
Location: Campus MIL. (To be confirmed in December.)

This is an in-person event. Should you be unable to attend in person, please, indicate this clearly upon submitting your abstract. (See below.)

08:00 - 12:00 Morning block
Details TBA after January 7.
12:00 - 13:00 Lunch
13:00 - 17:00 Afternoon block
Details TBA after January 7.
19:00 - Symposium
Location TBD in December.

Format

Graduate students, doctoral students and postdocs are invited to give a talk of the following three types.
  • Flash talks (5 minutes talk + 5 minutes discussion)
  • Technical presentations (10 + 5)
  • Demos (10 + 5)
Professors are invited to give a keynote talk of 15-20 minutes.

Call for participation

We invite every member of the research group to submit an abstract of 150-400 words. Abstracts should focus on one specific research topic and should include 5 keywords. We will use the abstracts to group talks into sessions and tracks in accordance with the focus areas of the group; and to publish them on the event's website.
Submit your abstracts by January 7 (Friday), 2022 by emailing it to the postdocs with the subject [GEODES Research Day and Symposium] Abstract.
