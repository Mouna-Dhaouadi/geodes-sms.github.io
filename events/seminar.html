The GEODES Seminar aims to bring together members of the group in a semi-formal setting with a semi-regular timing.

We'd especially love to see students at all levels reporting their progress, entertain presentations on core or adjacent topics, host dry-runs before conferences, etc. These events follow an in-person format. By that, we hope to re-establish the everyday social aspects of the research group in a post-COVID world.

Format: In person
Location: Pavillon André-Aisenstadt
