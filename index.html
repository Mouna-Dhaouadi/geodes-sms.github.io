GEODES Software Engineering Research Group

GEODES is the software engineering group of the University of Montreal, as part of the Department of Computer Cience and Operational Research (DIRO). The GEODES team now has more than 20 master's, doctoral and post-doctoral students. The team is led by three DIRO teachers: Michalis Famelis, Houari Sahraoui and Eugene Syriani. GEODES was founded as part of DIRO in 1992. Since then, more than a hundred students have obtained a master's degree and more than thirty have graduated with a PhD.
